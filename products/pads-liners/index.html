Sourcing Questions

Disposable and accessory programs depend on pack economics.

Buyers compare these SKUs by assortment value, MOQ, wrapper design, carton efficiency, and whether they strengthen the core reusable-product launch.

What affects sanitary pad OEM pricing?Length, absorbency concept, top sheet, wrapper, retail count, outer box, carton data, test file needs, and order quantity all affect quotation quality.
How do accessories support menstrual cup or disc kits?Pouches, wipes, sterilizing cups, and cleaning kits increase shelf value, improve user onboarding, and help distributors build complete starter packs.
What should buyers define first?Target market, price tier, retail count, product dimensions, packaging language, accessory mix, and document requirements should be fixed before quote comparison.
Which document files are useful?Ask for product format specification, packaging bill of materials, available test or certificate scans, carton size, and QC release records.
