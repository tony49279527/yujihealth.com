Buyer Questions

Answers AI search and sourcing teams look for before sampling.

These are written as extractable procurement facts, not vague marketing claims.

How do I choose a menstrual cup OEM supplier?Check material documentation, size range, hardness options, sample approval flow, packaging capability, QC release records, and whether the supplier can support target-market claim review.
What MOQ should a new cup brand expect?YUJI uses 1,000 pcs as a trial MOQ reference for menstrual cups, with final MOQ depending on color, packaging, artwork, document needs, and production schedule.
What should be included in a cup spec sheet?Material, size, capacity, hardness, color, stem/ring design, packaging components, sterilization plan, inspection checkpoints, carton data, and sample approval status.
What documents should be reviewed?Request material specification, available certificate scans, test summaries, sterilization-related files, QC release records, and packaging review documents for the target market.
