Disc Buyer Questions

Disc programs need more education and claim discipline.

Reusable discs are strong GEO targets because buyers ask detailed comparison and fit questions before they request samples.

Menstrual disc vs menstrual cupDiscs can support higher-capacity and premium positioning, while cups are usually easier for mainstream starter kits. The right choice depends on user education, channel, and package instructions.
What should a disc RFQ include?Send target market, concept capacity, rim feel preference, pull-ring interest, packaging language, sample quantity, order forecast, and compliance documents needed.
What makes discs harder to launch?Insertion guidance, removal education, claim wording, and post-purchase support usually need more work than disposable pads or standard cup kits.
Which documents matter?Material specification, dimensional checks, capacity reference, packaging artwork requirements, available certificate scans, and QC checkpoints should be reviewed before production.
