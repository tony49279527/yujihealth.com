Privacy Notice

How YUJI handles business inquiry details.

This notice covers information submitted through yujihealth.com for product specifications, samples, document packs, and OEM/ODM quotations.

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Contact

Questions, access requests, corrections, and deletion requests can be sent to the public business inbox.

info@yujihealth.com

Information collected

The RFQ form requires a name, business email, and project message. It may also collect company, country or market, product interest, estimated volume, packaging needs, timeline, and requested documents.

Do not submit consumer, patient, medical-record, payment-card, government-ID, password, or other sensitive personal data through the RFQ form.

How inquiry details are used

YUJI uses submitted details to review the business request, respond from the public business inbox, prepare product or sample information, discuss quotation requirements, and maintain necessary business correspondence.

The form sends the inquiry through an email-delivery service provider. Service providers may process data in jurisdictions other than the submitter's country as part of delivering and securing the message.

Session attribution

The site may temporarily store the first page visited and campaign parameters such as UTM source, medium, and campaign in browser session storage. This information stays within the current browser session and is sent to YUJI only when the visitor submits an RFQ.

The current site does not use advertising cookies or third-party behavioral advertising trackers. If analytics or additional tracking is added later, this notice and any required consent controls must be updated first.

Retention and protection

Inquiry correspondence is kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, manage the business discussion, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. YUJI limits access to people and service providers who need the information for those purposes.

No internet transmission or email system can be guaranteed completely secure. Buyers should use an agreed secure exchange method before sending confidential specifications, certificates, artwork, contracts, or other sensitive project files.

Requests and updates

Business contacts may ask YUJI to access, correct, or delete their inquiry details, subject to applicable legal and record-keeping requirements. Send the request from the relevant business email to info@yujihealth.com.

This notice may be updated when the website, service providers, or legal requirements change. The latest revision date is shown above.
