OEM / ODM Path

A clear project path from brief to export handoff.

The OEM workflow explains sample validation, material and packaging choices, trial production, QC release, export cartons, and the information buyers should provide for a fast quotation.

01

Brief & sample

Target market, channel, product format, volume, packaging, and compliance needs are confirmed before sampling.

02

Customize & approve

Use existing structures for speed or develop custom colors, molds, pouches, boxes, labels, and instruction inserts.

03

Trial production

Sample lots verify dimensions, hardness, packaging fit, sterilization plan, and buyer approval records.

04

Mass delivery

Production, QC sampling, release documents, carton labeling, and export handoff are managed as one path.
