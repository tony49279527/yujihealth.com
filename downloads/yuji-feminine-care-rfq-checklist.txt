YUJI Feminine Care OEM RFQ Checklist
Last reviewed: 2026-07-09

Send this information when requesting specs, samples, MOQ, document review, or OEM/ODM pricing.

1. Buyer and market
- Company name
- Contact person and email
- Target country or region
- Sales channel: Amazon, DTC, distributor, pharmacy, retail, or mixed
- Preferred reply timeline

2. Product scope
- Product line: menstrual cups, menstrual discs, sanitary pads, liners, wipes, pouches, cleaning kits, or mixed program
- Size, capacity, absorbency, material, formula, color, hardness, rim, stem, pull-ring, or pack direction
- Existing product reference or target photos, if available
- Whether the project uses an existing structure or needs custom development

3. Volume and timeline
- Sample quantity
- Trial-order quantity
- Forecast order quantity
- Expected launch date
- Reorder cycle, if known

4. Packaging
- Storage pouch, retail box, wrapper, leaflet, barcode, carton, and label needs
- Instruction language
- Artwork status: ready, draft, or needs support
- Private-label requirements
- Shelf or channel constraints

5. Documents and compliance review
- Specification sheet needed
- Material or product test files needed
- Available certificate scans needed
- QC release or inspection records needed
- Sterilization-related files needed, if relevant
- Target-market claim or label requirements to verify

6. Shipping and handoff
- Incoterms preference
- Destination country and port or warehouse city
- Carton mark requirements
- Packing list or export document requirements
- Freight preference

Important boundary:
YUJI can provide available product, process, certificate, and QC records for buyer review. Final market approval, registration, importer responsibility, retail compliance, label wording, and product claims must be verified by the buyer for the target country.
