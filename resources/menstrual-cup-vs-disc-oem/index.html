Menstrual cup vs disc OEM: which reusable program fits the launch?

Buyers often ask for both cup and disc quotes before they decide. This page compares capacity, education load, packaging, MOQ logic, and channel fit so the RFQ can start with a clear product choice.

Last reviewed: July 10, 2026

Buyer Summary

Cups are usually easier to launch; discs need more education.

Menstrual cups are the mainstream reusable silicone starter SKU for DTC, Amazon, pharmacy pilots, and kit programs. Menstrual discs can support higher-capacity and premium positioning, but they usually need clearer insertion and removal education, stronger claim discipline, and more packaging support.

Cup capacity20-30 ml mainstream reference range with S/M/L sizing.
Disc capacity50-65 ml reference range with rim and pull-ring options.
Cup MOQ1,000 pcs trial MOQ reference, subject to color and packaging.
Disc MOQReviewed by project based on structure, color, and pack plan.

Side-by-side OEM comparison

Decision point Menstrual cups Menstrual discs
Best channel fit DTC, Amazon, retail starter kits, pharmacy pilots Premium reusable launches and education-led markets
User education load Moderate; sizing and insertion guidance still matter Higher; insertion, removal, and claim wording need more care
Customization focus Size, hardness, color, logo, pouch, box, leaflet Rim feel, pull-ring design, color, kit packaging
Document focus Material, biocompatibility-oriented records, EO plan, QC release Material, dimensional checks, packaging fit, market claim review
Launch risk Lower for first reusable SKU Higher if packaging and education are incomplete

How to choose before requesting samples

  1. Define the hero SKU. If the brand needs a mainstream reusable entry product, start with cups. If the brand wants premium capacity positioning and can educate users, review discs.
  2. Check packaging readiness. Cups often launch with pouch, box, and leaflet kits. Discs usually need clearer instruction design and claim review.
  3. Confirm MOQ logic. Cup programs can use a 1,000 pcs trial reference. Disc MOQ depends more on structure, color, and packaging complexity.
  4. Align documents early. Request material specs, available certificate scans, QC checkpoints, and packaging review files before artwork lock.
  5. Decide whether to dual-SKU. Some buyers launch cups first, then add discs after the reusable category is established.

RFQ fields that make cup vs disc quotes comparable

Send target market, channel, preferred product format, capacity expectation, color, packaging language, sample quantity, first-order forecast, and document list. Without these fields, cup and disc quotes cannot be compared fairly.
