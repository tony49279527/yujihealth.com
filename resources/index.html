FAQ

Common buyer questions before sampling.

Are menstrual cups safe?

YUJI cup programs use medical-grade silicone and can be supported by biocompatibility-oriented testing records for buyer review. Buyers must verify final claims and market rules before commercial use.

How should buyers choose cup sizes?

S often targets lighter flow or younger users, M is a balanced mainstream size, and L supports higher-flow or postpartum positioning. Final naming should match product data and local claim rules.

Can YUJI provide samples?

Yes. Sample packs can be prepared for product, packaging, and market-fit review. Express freight is normally paid by the buyer.

What is the normal lead time?

Regular orders are typically planned around 15-20 working days after approval; urgent programs may be reviewed case by case.

Can packaging and formulas be customized?

Yes. Existing structures support lower-risk launch speed; custom molds, colors, packs, and selected formulations can be developed for larger programs.

Which documents can buyers request before ordering?

Specification sheets, available certificate scans, test summaries, QC release records, sterilization-related documents, packaging review files, and carton references can be prepared according to product and market.
