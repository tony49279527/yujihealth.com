MOQ Guide

Menstrual cup OEM MOQ: what changes the trial order number.

YUJI uses 1,000 pcs as a trial MOQ reference for menstrual cups. Final MOQ still depends on size mix, color, packaging, artwork, document needs, and the launch schedule.

Last reviewed: July 10, 2026

Buyer Summary

Use the 1,000 pcs reference as a starting point, not a fixed quote.

A useful cup MOQ answer starts with the launch model. Buyers who reuse an existing cup structure, keep packaging simple, and approve samples quickly can usually move faster than buyers who need new colors, custom molds, multi-language retail boxes, or broad document packs.

Trial MOQ1,000 pcs reference for menstrual cup programs.
Lead timeTypically 15-20 working days after approvals.
Size mixS/M/L mix affects production planning and carton logic.
DocumentsSpecs, certificate scans, QC release, and packaging files should be listed early.

What changes menstrual cup MOQ?

  1. Cup structure. Existing Classic, Soft, or Sport options quote faster than new mold development.
  2. Size mix. A single size trial is different from a balanced S/M/L assortment for retail or Amazon kits.
  3. Color and logo. Custom colors and logo placement change material planning and approval steps.
  4. Packaging complexity. Pouch-only packs differ from retail box, leaflet, barcode, sterilizing cup, and starter-kit programs.
  5. Sample approval path. Fit, hardness, packaging copy, and artwork should be confirmed before larger production.
  6. Compliance scope. Destination-market document needs can affect timing even when the unit MOQ stays similar.

RFQ information that produces a real cup quote

Send target country, channel, cup sizes, hardness preference, color, logo placement, pouch or box structure, instruction language, sample quantity, launch date, first-order forecast, and document list. Without these fields, the supplier can only return a generic range.

RFQ field Why it matters Example buyer input
Target market Controls label language and claim risk EU DTC, English and German packaging
Size mix Affects production and carton planning S 20% / M 50% / L 30%
Pack structure Changes packaging MOQ and lead time Box, pouch, leaflet, sterilizing cup
Document needs Prevents late compliance surprises Specs, available certificate scans, QC release

Recommended path for a first cup launch

Start with available cup structures and a clear size mix. Request samples with packaging direction, then confirm hardness, color, artwork, and document scope before locking the trial order. This sequence makes the 1,000 pcs MOQ reference more practical and reduces rework.
