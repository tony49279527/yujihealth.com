RFQ Checklist

Feminine care OEM compliance and quotation checklist.

A clear RFQ helps YUJI return product specs, sample options, MOQ references, packaging paths, and document packs that match the buyer's market instead of a generic quote.

Last reviewed: July 8, 2026

Use this before emailing

  • Define product line and market
  • Prepare volume and timeline
  • List packaging requirements
  • State certificate or test needs
  • Clarify sample and approval process
  • Confirm shipping and carton requirements

Checklist

Eight fields every serious RFQ should include.

Market
Country, language, sales channel, importer role, and claim restrictions.
Product
Menstrual cups, discs, pads, liners, intimate care, pouches, wipes, cleaning kits, or mixed programs.
Specification
Size, capacity, material, hardness, formula direction, color, accessory mix, or retail count.
Volume
Sample quantity, trial order, forecast order, and reorder cycle.
Packaging
Storage pouch, retail box, wrapper, leaflet, barcode, carton, label, and language needs.
Documents
Specification sheet, certificate scans, test summaries, QC release, sterilization-related files, and carton data.
Timeline
Sample deadline, artwork approval date, production target, and launch window.
Shipping
Incoterms, destination, carton marks, packing list needs, and freight preference.

Compliance boundary for buyers

YUJI can prepare available records for review, but final market approval, registration, label wording, importer obligations, and product claims must be verified by the buyer for the target country. This boundary should be stated early to avoid unsafe marketing claims.

Next step

Use the checklist to send a complete email to info@yujihealth.com. For a faster response, attach artwork drafts, target product photos, reference packaging, and any required standard or certificate list.
